Description

The Space

Vacation rental "Le Mura 4" is a cosy and welcoming one-bedded room apartment located on the first floor of a building with few units. It offers entrance on living / dining room with kitchen corner with central isle, master bedroom with queen size bed and bathroom with shower. The sofa in the living room turns into a double bed. Upon request we can provide a free baby cot (children aged 0 -3). With A/C and free internet wi-fi. Stunning view over the Town Walls. Located in a envyable position, close to all main sightseeings but far off the crowd. Close to free parking lots.

Guest Access

Guests have at their complete disposal the whole apartment and can use all aplliances.

Interaction with Guests

Upon arrival, a member of the Itaco Staff will meet you for the keys handover. During your stay we are only one knock away since our office is located in the same building. We are also always available over the phone.

The Neighbourhood

Only 200 mt from the main street named Via Fillungo where all shops are; 200 mt from the famous Piazza Anfiteatro.

Getting Around

Via Buiamonti is a short street between Porta San Iacopo and Porta Santa Maria (two of the main gates). The closest parking lot is in Piazza Santa Maria (with fee between 8 am and 8 pm - 1,50 Euro/hour - within blue lined car places) while the closest free parking lots (within white lined car places) are just outside the Walls, in Via Iacopo della Quercia or in Via delle Tagliate.

Other Things to Note

Free internet wi.fi.
